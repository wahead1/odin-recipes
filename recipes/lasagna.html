Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta that is made with layers of lasagna noodles, cheese, and meat.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large pot, cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned and crumbly. Drain excess fat.
  4. Mix tomato sauce, salt, and pepper into ground beef mixture. Simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.
